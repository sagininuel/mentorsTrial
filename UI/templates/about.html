About Us

Free Mentors is a social initiative where accomplished professionals become role models to young people to provide free mentorship sessions. This platform therefore guides you through a comprehensive session on how to connect with your mentor of choice. The aim is to provide you with all the necessary tools you may need to route you to your preferred career destination.

Grab your chance! Now is the time for you to shine as well. Sign up and book a session with your preferred mentor.
